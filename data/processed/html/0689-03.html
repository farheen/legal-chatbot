William E. DeMAINE et al. v. Louis CEDRONE et al.

No. 79-339-A.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

July 23, 1981.

F. Monroe Allen, Providence, for plaintiffs.

Anthony M. Gallone, Providence, for defendants.

ORDER

This case is assigned to the show cause calendar.

The plaintiffs are directed to appear on a date to be assigned and show cause why their appeal should not be dismissed in light of their failure to demonstrate any error in the decision of the trial justice.

WEISBERGER and SHEA, JJ., did not participate.
