COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania, Respondent, v. Michael BARONI, Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Sept. 19, 2002.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 19th day of September, 2002, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is granted, limited to

Whether the jury’s guilty verdict is vitiated and non-existent due to the trial court’s failure to instruct the jury on the beyond-a-reasonable-doubt standard of proof required by the corpus delicti rule, and whether Petitioner’s petition for post conviction collateral relief is time-barred by the provisions of 42 Pa. C.S. § 9545(b)(1).
