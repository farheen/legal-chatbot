Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

Nov. 19, 1981.

Arthur G. Capaldi, Coventry, for plaintiff.

Gunning, LaFazia & Gnys, Inc., Richard T. Linn, Providence, for defendants.

ORDER

The plaintiff’s motion to remand is granted and this case is hereby remanded to the Superior Court for reconsideration in light of this court’s opinion in Pin Pin H. Su v. Kemper Insurance Companies et al, R.I., 431 A.2d 416 (1981).
