Pauline ANDRADE v. STATE.

No. 80-185-A.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

May 5, 1980.

Nugent & Nugent, J. Joseph Nugent Jr., Providence, for plaintiff.

Dennis J. Roberts II, Atty. Gen., Harold E. Krause, Special Asst. Atty. Gen., for defendant.

ORDER

The parties herein are hereby authorized to stipulate to a judgment in favor of plaintiff in the sum of $50,000. The state may continue to press its appeal with respect to the propriety of the trial justice’s refusal to eliminate the interest on said judgment in light of the provisions of Gen. Laws of 1956 (1969 Reenactment) § 9-31-2.
