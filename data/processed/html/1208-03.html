In the Matter of Romaine PHILLIPS. Petition for Reinstatement.

No. 611 Disciplinary Docket No. 2.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

June 19, 2002.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 19th day of June, 2002, the Petition for Reinstatement is denied *1209and petitioner is forever barred from the practice of law in the Commonwealth of Pennsylvania. See Office of Disciplinary Counsel v. Keller, 509 Pa. 573, 506 A.2d 872 (1986). Pursuant to Rule 218(e), Pa. R.D.E., petitioner is directed to pay the expenses incurred by the Board in the investigation and processing of the Petition for Reinstatement.

Mr. Justice Castille did not participate in this matter.
