Christine D. CHASE and Leroy G. Chase, Petitioners v. NATIONAL FUEL GAS CORPORATION, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

June 1, 1998.

AMENDED ORDER

PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 1st day of June, 1998, we GRANT the Petition for Allowance of Appeal and we REVERSE the order of the Superior Court. We REMAND the matter to the Court of Common Pleas of Erie County consistent with this Court’s opinion in Jacobs v. Halloran, — Pa. -, 710 A.2d 1098 (1998) and Marino v. Hackman, — Pa. -, 710 A.2d 1108 (1998).

SAYLOR, J., did not participate in the consideration or decision of this matter.
