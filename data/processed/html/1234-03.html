Ronald BELL et al. v. ZONING BOARD OF REVIEW OF the CITY OF EAST PROVIDENCE et al.

No. 79-142-Appeal.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

Nov. 29, 1979.

Anthony E. Grilli, Providence, for plaintiffs.

Robert R. Nocera, Asst. Sol., East Providence, Joseph B. Carty, Jr., Providence, for defendants.

ORDER

This case is assigned to the February, 1980 show cause calendar. The plaintiffs are directed to appear and show cause why this appeal should not be dismissed since review of a Superior Court judgment in a zoning case can only be obtained by petition for prerogative writ of certiorari. Bassi v. *1235 Zoning Board of Review, 107 R.I. 702, 271 A.2d 210 (1970).

DORIS, J., did not participate.
