STATE ex rel. Aram K. BERBERIAN v. Thomas J. PAOLINO.

No. 81-408-Appeal.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

March 18, 1983.

Michael Kiselica, Cranston, for plaintiff.

Robert S. Bruzzi, Providence, for defendant.

ORDER

This matter was argued before the court on March 11, 1983, on our order to the plaintiff to show cause why his appeal should not be dismissed. After consideration of briefs and oral arguments, it is the conclusion of the court that no cause has been shown.

Therefore, the plaintiffs appeal is denied and dismissed, and the judgment appealed from is affirmed.
