Oliver P. Swope, Appellant, v. Harper Williams, Appellee.

Division B.

Decision filed January 12, 1931.

Maguire & Voorhis, for Appellant;

Giles & Gurney, for Appellee.

Per Curiam.

This cause having heretofore been submitted to the Court upon the transcript of the record of the order herein, and briefs and argument of counsel for the *1702respective parties, and the records having been seen and inspected, and the Court being now advised of its judgment to be given in the premises, it seems to the Court that there is no error in the said order; it is, therefore, considered, ordered and adjudged by the Court that the said order of the circuit court be, and the same is hereby, affirmed.

Whitfield, P. J., and Strum: and Buford, J. J., concur.
