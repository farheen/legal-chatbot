Violet M. CAVANAGH v. Robert D. CAVANAGH.

No. 79-17-A.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

Dec. 13, 1979.

John H. Hines, Jr., Providence, for petitioner.

Robert D. Cavanagh, pro se.

ORDER

The motion to remand this case to the Family Court for hearing on the co-commissioners’ and co-receivers’ petition for instructions is granted. Following said hearing, the papers in this case shall be returned to this court forthwith.

DORIS, J., did not participate.
