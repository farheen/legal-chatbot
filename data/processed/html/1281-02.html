PER CURIAM.

Based upon our disposition in Commonwealth v. Williams, — Pa.-, 733 A.2d 593 (1999), we affirm the Order of the Erie County Court of Common Pleas dated May 27, 1998. Furthermore, we remand the *1282matter to the common pleas court for disposition of any remaining issues.
