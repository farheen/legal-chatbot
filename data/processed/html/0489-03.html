In the Matter of Charles George STINNER Petition for Reinstatement from Inactive Status.

No. 22 DB 2006.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

June 23, 2006.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 23rd day of June, 2006, the Report and Recommendations of the Disciplinary Board of the Supreme Court of Pennsylvania dated May 9, 2006, are approved and it is ORDERED that Charles George Stinner, who has been on inactive status, has never been suspended or disbarred, and has demonstrated that *490he has the moral qualifications, competency and learning in law required for admission to practice in the Commonwealth, shall be and is hereby reinstated to active status as a member of the Bar of this Commonwealth. The expenses incurred by the Board in the investigation and processing of the Petition for Reinstatement shall be paid by the Petitioner.
