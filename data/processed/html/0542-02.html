BELVOIR ASSOCIATES et al. v. Anthony J. BOVE d/b/a Mirabar Lounge.

No. 80-376-A.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

Jan. 22, 1981.

Hinckley, Allen, Salisbury & Parsons, Howard E. Walker, Providence, for plaintiffs.

Gunning, LaFazia & Gnys, Inc., Netti C. Vogel, Providence, for defendant.

ORDER

The plaintiff’s motion to dismiss this appeal pursuant to Rule 16(g), as prayed, is denied. This case is assigned to the show cause calendar. The parties are directed to appear and show cause why defendant’s appeal should not be sustained in view of the amended provisions of G.L. of 1956 (1969 Reenactment) § 9-12-10.
