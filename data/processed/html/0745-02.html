Longolucco, Lenihan & Orsinger, John M. McLoughlin, Wakefield, for petitioner.

ORDER

The respondent is directed to file his answer to the petition for writ of habeas corpus and therein to show cause, if any he has, why the petition should not be granted, said answer to be made in compliance with the provisions of Rule 14.
