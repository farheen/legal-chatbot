Aram BERBERIAN v. Clarence I. MITCHELL.

No. 79-449-A.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

Nov. 19, 1981.

J. Ronald Fishbein, Providence, for plaintiff.

William J. Toohey, City Sol., Warwick, for respondent.

ORDER

This ease is assigned to the show cause calendar.

The petitioner is directed to appear on a date to be assigned and show cause why his appeal should not be dismissed in view of the fact that his raising of the issue of punitive damages is apparently barred by the doctrine of res judicata.
