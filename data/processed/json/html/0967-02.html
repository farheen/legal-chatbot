Lewis E. ATKINSON, Petitioner, v. James Michael EVANS, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

May 16, 2002.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 16th day of May 2002, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED, limited to the issue of whether a cause of action for intentional interference with contractual relations is viable in a marital context in Pennsylvania.
