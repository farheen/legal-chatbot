No. 82-237-M.P.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

June 3, 1983.

Larry Dub, Providence, for plaintiff.

ORDER

Since the plaintiffs’ motion to affirm was denied on the merits, and not because of any procedural defect, plaintiffs’ motion for reconsideration is denied.
