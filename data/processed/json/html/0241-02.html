James E. CORREIA, Jr. v. DEPARTMENT OF EMPLOYMENT SECURITY.

No. 77-224-Appeal.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

Dec. 11, 1980.

Lovett & Linder, Ltd., Lauren E. Jones, Providence, for plaintiff.

Charles H. McLaughlin, Providence, for defendant.

ORDER

This matter is before the court on an order directing the plaintiff to show cause why his appeal should not be dismissed since the trial justice apparently did not abuse his discretion in awarding a counsel fee. No cause having been shown to us, we hereby dismiss the appeal.
