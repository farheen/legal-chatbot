MOUNTAIN VILLAGE, Respondent v. The BOARD OF SUPERVISORS OF LONGSWAMP TOWNSHIP, Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

May 11, 2004.

ORDER

PER CURIAM.

AND NOW, this 11th day of May, 2004, the petition for allowance of appeal is GRANTED. The parties are directed to address the following issue: *833Whether the Commonwealth Court correctly determined that pursuant to the Municipalities Planning Code, 53 P.S. §§ 10503 and 10510, legal fees incurred by a municipality reviewing a land development plan cannot be assessed against a developer-applicant.
