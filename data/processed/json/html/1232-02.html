In the Matter of Miguel R. HERNANDEZ.

No. 2011-078-M.P.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

June 3, 2011.

Miguel R. Hernandez.

David D. Curtin.

ORDER

On March 8, 2011 this court suspended Miguel R. Hernandez, the petitioner, from the practice of law for his failure to comply with an order of the court that he file an answer to two disciplinary complaints. On May 13, 2011, he filed a petition for reinstatement. This matter came before the court at its conference on June 1, 2011. The petitioner appeared before the court, without counsel.

Having reviewed the petition, the response of disciplinary counsel, and having heard the representations of the petitioner, we deny the petition without prejudice. The petitioner may not submit a new reinstatement petition until three months from the date of this order.
