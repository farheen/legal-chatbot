Robert L. THEYS, Petitioner, v. Jules PATT, Individually and the Patt Organization, Respondent.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

May 5, 1998.

ORDER

AND NOW, this 5th day of May, 1998, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal is GRANTED. The Order of the Superior Court is VACATED pursuant to Jacobs v. Hallaran, — Pa. -, — A.2d -, 1998 WL 149478 (Pa.1998), and the matter is REMANDED to the Court of Common Pleas for proceedings consistent with our Jacobs decision.

SAYLOR, J., did not participate in the consideration or decision of this matter.
