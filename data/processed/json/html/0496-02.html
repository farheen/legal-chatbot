Lori Beth DURFEE et al. v. COLONIAL PENN INSURANCE COMPANY et al.

No. 82-579-A.

Supreme Court of Rhode Island.

April 29, 1983.

Sheila Tobie Swan, Vivian Tseng, Providence, for plaintiff.

Robert J. Dumouchel, Providence, John J. Finan, Pawtucket, for defendant.

ORDER

The motion of defendant Rhode Island Insurance Plan Service Center to affirm the judgment of the Superior Court pursuant to Rule 16(g) is granted. The motion of defendant Colonial Penn Insurance Company for affirmance of the Superior Court ruling is denied.

Weisberger, J., did not participate.
