ORDER

The defendants’ motions to dismiss this appeal for lack of jurisdiction are denied. The defendants’ motions to remand this case to the Superior Court for hearing on their motions for summary judgment are granted. Following said hearing, the papers herein shall be returned to this court forthwith.
