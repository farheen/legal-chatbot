Carolyne Y. HYNES
v.
Sharon M. JONES

Supreme Court of Connecticut.

Decided October 25, 2017

Michael P. Kaelin and William N. Wright, in support of the petition.

The plaintiffs petition for certification to appeal from the Appellate Court, 175 Conn.App. 80, 167 A.3d 375 (2017), is granted, limited to the following question:

"Did the Appellate Court properly conclude that a September 11th Victim Compensation Fund award, paid to a surviving spouse as a representative payee for the benefit of her minor child, was subject to the jurisdiction and control of Connecticut Probate Courts?"
