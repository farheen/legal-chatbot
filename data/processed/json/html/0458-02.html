ORDER

AND NOW, this 16th day of April, 1997, the Petition for Allowance of Appeal at 377 W.D. Allocatur Docket 1996, is granted, limited to the issue of what test should be applied to determine whether a business entity is a public utility for zoning purposes where the zoning ordinance does not define that term. Accordingly, we DENY these Petitions for Allowance of Appeal concerning the remaining issues and parties.
