COMMONWEALTH of Pennsylvania v. Robert E. LASSEN, Petitioner.

Supreme Court of Pennsylvania.

Feb. 4, 1994.

Before NIX, C.J., and FLAHERTY, ZAPPALA, CAPPY, CASTILLE and MONTEMURO, JJ.

ORDER

“PER CURIAM:

AND NOW, this 1st day of February, 1994, the request to proceed in forma pau-peris is granted and the Motion for Extension of Time to File Petition for Allocatur and request for appointment of counsel are denied.
